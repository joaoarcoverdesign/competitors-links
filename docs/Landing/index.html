FAQ

Common questions.

What is an "unlimited request"?
As many requests in your backlog as you want. We work through them one at a time. No monthly cap on how many get completed.
What counts as a request?
One clearly scoped deliverable — a screen redesign, a landing page, a set of dashboard cards, an onboarding flow. Large projects are broken into multiple requests.
How long does each request take?
Most requests: 48–72 business hours. Complex multi-screen flows may take longer — your designer always confirms the timeline before starting.
Can I pause or cancel?
Yes. Pause for up to 2 months (billing stops). Cancel anytime from your client hub — no call required, no questions asked.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. 14 days, no credit card. After that, subscribe or leave — no obligation.
What timezone?
LATAM-aligned (UTC-3 to UTC-5). 0–3 hours of overlap with US East Coast — better than Eastern European alternatives (6–9h difference).
Do you support Portuguese and Spanish?
Yes. All communication and deliverables in EN, PT, or ES. Your designer is fluent in your language.
Is UX research the same as a design request?
No. Your monthly research session (usability testing, user interviews, heuristic review) is separate from and in addition to your design requests.
